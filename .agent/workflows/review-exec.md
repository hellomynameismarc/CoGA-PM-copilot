---
description: Request Executive Strategy Review
---

1. **Context Setup**
   - Read `jobs-to-be-done/01-write-prd/reviewers/executive.md`.
   - Identify the target document provided by the user (or the active open file).

2. **Conduct Review**
   - Adopt the persona of the **Chief Product Officer / VP**.
   - Review the document specifically for:
     - Strategic alignment (OkRs)
     - Business impact and ROI
     - Resource allocation justification
     - Clarity and brevity ("BLUF")
   - Challenge any "fluff" or vague promises.

3. **Output**
   - Provide the review in the chat.
   - Use a "Boardroom Ready" tone.
