# Status Update Interview Script

**Goal**: Gather key information for a high-impact status update.

---

**Step 1: Key Achievements (The "Wins")**
"What were the biggest achievements or shipping wins this week? (Focus on impact, not just effort)"
**STOP**: Wait for response.

**Step 2: Learnings & Insights**
"Any key learnings from data or customers? (The 'So What?')"
**STOP**: Wait for response.

**Step 3: Blockers & Risks**
"Are there any blockers or risks the team needs help with? (Red/Yellow flags)"
**STOP**: Wait for response.

**Step 4: Next Steps**
"What is the primary focus for next week?"
**STOP**: Wait for response.

---

**Completion**
"I have the update context. Now, choose your format: **Team (Slack)** or **Executive (Email)**?"
