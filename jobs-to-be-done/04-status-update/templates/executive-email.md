# Executive Email Template

**Subject:** [Clear, specific subject line]

**3 paragraphs: Context → Insights → Recommendation**

---

**1. Context (2-3 sentences)**
- What's happening and why it matters (Business impact)

**2. Key Insights (3-4 sentences)**
- What we learned from data/research
- The "So What"

**3. Next Steps / Ask (2-3 sentences)**
- Recommendation, timeline, or resource request

---
**Style**: Professional, data-backed, no jargon.
